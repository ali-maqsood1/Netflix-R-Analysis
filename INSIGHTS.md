# Key Findings

## Dataset Overview

- 8,807 Netflix titles analyzed.
- Majority of content consists of movies.
- United States contributes the highest number of titles.
- Drama and International Movies are the most common genres.
- TV-MA is the most frequent content rating.
- Most movies fall between 80–120 minutes.
- Netflix's content library grew rapidly after 2015.
- Several metadata fields (director, cast, country) contain missing values.
